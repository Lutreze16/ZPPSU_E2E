ZPPSU
Enrollment to Employment
System

The ZPPSU Enrollment to Employment (E2E) system is an innovative and comprehensive platform created to easily connect academic endeavors with career success. It acts as a living ecosystem, supporting students from the moment of enrollment all the way through their academic journey and, in the end, easing their transition into employment.
